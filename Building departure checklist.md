# If you are the last to leave, please check the following:

1. **Around inside building**
    1. Are you really the last? Check the back rooms.
    1. As you do, check all heaters and lights are off (except the kitchen). There are heaters in the Bertrand Russel Room (lounge) and in the corner by the laser cutter.
    1. Check that the glass door to the old front area is locked.
	1. Check that powered tools such as 3D printers and the laser cutter are turned off.
	1. Verify that you've left no open food containers or dirty utensils etc. (there are mice around.)
1. **At the door**
    1. Check that the container door is closed.
    1. Press the big red button. 
    	- You have 2 minutes to go out and shut the door.
1. **When you get outside**
    1. Ensure that the door is pulled shut behind you **and locked**
    1. Close the chain across the gateway.
