# This machine must not be used by anyone who is not a Hackerspace-approved Operator

If in doubt, please contact the machine manager: Mark Pearson [mark.pearson@hobarthackerspace.org.au](mailto:mark.pearson@hobarthackerspace.org.au)

# Conditions of Use

## Machine Operators

- No person may operate the CNC machine unless they:
	1. are an [approved operator](CNC_Operator_Approval) operating within the conditions of their approval or 
    1. are operating under the direct supervision of an approved operator.
- The machine operator is responsible to ensure the safety of all others in the vicinity.
	- At the minimum, this includes:
    	- Remaining at a safe distance
		- Wearing of appropriate PPE inculding eye protection.
- Operators must comply with all conditions in the [CNC Operation Requirements](CNC_Operation_Requirements)
- Operators must always wear appropriate PPE whilst using the machine.

## Jobs

- All g-code files must have been verified by a simulator or other methods before being run on the machine.
